ABOUT ENHYPEN



ENHYPEN is the first boyband created by BELIFT LAB, a joint venture between HYBE and CJ ENM, consisting of seven multinational members JUNGWON, HEESEUNG, JAY, JAKE, SUNGHOON, SUNOO and NI-KI. They are the finalists of global music competition show ‘I-LAND,’ which finished airing in September 2020. The members have already shown infinite possibilities and growth not only as individuals, but also as a team. Similar to the ‘hyphen(-)’ that connects one word to another, ENHYPEN means that seven different boys connect to discover one another and grow together. It also indicates their ambitious plan to connect people and the world through their music.


I want to express my affection, for Enhypen with all my heart. I am passionately in love with this group that I effortlessly mention their names in any conversation. Even though I have been a fan of KPOP groups in the past my connection with Enhypen is truly special. My journey with them started before their survival show. I have been there for every challenge and success witnessing their unwavering dedication and tireless efforts. For me Enhypen represents the point of my journey and I can confidently say that no other groups will sway me in the future. They have become my sanctuary, a place where I feel safe and whenever the opportunity arises I will proudly praise them because they genuinely deserve all the love and admiration they receive. Enhypen is more than a group to me; they are like a home within the world of KPOP. There are no limits to my love, for them.
